Building Lasting Partnerships Through Textile Excellence

White Heaven Linen combines technical expertise, trusted international manufacturing partnerships and a commitment to quality to deliver premium textile solutions that create lasting value for customers worldwide.

Who We Are

White Heaven Linen™ is an Ireland-based supplier of premium textile solutions dedicated to delivering exceptional quality, innovation and long-term value. Working with carefully selected international manufacturing partners, we combine textile engineering expertise, responsible sourcing and rigorous quality assurance to develop products that consistently meet the highest international expectations. Rather than simply supplying textiles, we build trusted partnerships by providing dependable service, transparent communication and tailored solutions that support our customers' long-term success.

Our Story

White Heaven Linen was established with a vision of bringing together textile expertise, international manufacturing capabilities and customer-focused service. Our experience across textile engineering, product development, quality management and global sourcing enables us to understand not only the products we supply but also the operational challenges our customers face. This knowledge allows us to deliver solutions that combine quality, performance and long-term reliability while building lasting business relationships based on trust and professionalism.

Quality Without Compromise

Customer-Centred Approach
