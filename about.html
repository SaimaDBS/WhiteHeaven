About White Heaven Linen™

Built on textile engineering expertise, trusted manufacturing partnerships and a commitment to delivering premium textile solutions with quality, responsibility and lasting value.

Who We Are

White Heaven Linen™ is the premium textile brand of MSaleemS, an Ireland-based company specialising in sourcing, developing and supplying premium textile solutions through trusted international manufacturing partnerships.

Our approach combines textile engineering expertise with responsible sourcing and rigorous quality standards to deliver products that provide comfort, durability and dependable performance.

Rather than simply supplying textiles, we work closely with customers to understand their requirements and provide carefully developed solutions that support long-term success.

Our Story

White Heaven Linen™ was established with a clear purpose—to combine technical textile expertise with trusted manufacturing partnerships in order to deliver dependable textile solutions for modern businesses.

Our experience within textile engineering, quality assurance and international sourcing enables us to understand customer expectations, identify the right manufacturing partners and provide products that balance quality, functionality and value.

As markets continue to evolve, we remain committed to building trusted relationships through integrity, transparency and continuous improvement.
