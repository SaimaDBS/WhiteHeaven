About White Heaven Linen

Delivering premium textile solutions through quality, innovation, sustainability and trusted global partnerships.

Who We Are

White Heaven Linen is an Ireland-based supplier of premium healthcare, hospitality and institutional textile solutions. We work with carefully selected international manufacturing partners to supply high-quality textile products that meet European performance, durability and sustainability expectations. Our expertise combines textile engineering, quality assurance, product development and responsible sourcing to deliver reliable solutions for demanding professional environments.

Our Journey

Our Beginning

White Heaven Linen was established with the vision of supplying premium textile solutions that combine quality, comfort and durability for professional markets.

Our Growth

By developing strong international partnerships, we expanded our product portfolio to serve healthcare, hospitality and institutional customers across Europe.

Today

Today we continue to focus on innovation, customer satisfaction, quality assurance and sustainable textile development.
