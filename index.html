Welcome to White Heaven Linen

White Heaven Linen is an Ireland-based supplier of premium textile solutions serving healthcare, hospitality, commercial laundry and institutional sectors. Our products are developed through trusted international manufacturing partnerships and are designed to deliver exceptional durability, industrial washing performance, comfort and sustainability. By combining textile engineering expertise, rigorous quality assurance and responsible sourcing, we provide customers with reliable textile solutions that meet the highest international expectations.
