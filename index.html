Welcome to White Heaven Linen™

Premium Textile Solutions Designed for Modern Businesses

White Heaven Linen™ is an Ireland-based textile company committed to delivering premium-quality home textiles, hospitality linen, healthcare textiles and technical textile solutions. We work with carefully selected manufacturing partners to provide products that combine exceptional craftsmanship, durability and responsible sourcing.

Textile Expertise with Global Reach

Combining years of experience in textile manufacturing, product development and sustainability, White Heaven Linen™ partners with hotels, healthcare organisations, retailers and private-label brands seeking dependable textile solutions.

Every collection is developed with a focus on quality, functionality and long-term performance while supporting responsible manufacturing practices and transparent sourcing.
